Particle Review

What is the "Direct Object" particle?

を (o)

We will use this a lot today!

You don't need complex verbs to order. Just use the ultimate command word: ください (Kudasai).

The Plug-and-Play Formula

[ Item ]
を
ください。

"Please give me [Item]."

みず を ください。

Mizu o kudasai (Water, please).

メニュー を ください。

Menyuu o kudasai (Menu, please).

Your Order

すし を ひとつ ください。

Sushi o hitotsu kudasai.

To say you want something, use the word ほしい (Hoshii). But there is a logical trap!

The "Ga" Trap

In Japanese, "Hoshii" (want) is treated as an Adjective, not a verb! Therefore, it cannot take the Object marker を (o). It must take the Spotlight marker が (ga).

みず が ほしい です。

Mizu ga hoshii desu (I want water).
