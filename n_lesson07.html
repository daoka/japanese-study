The "Kaerimasu" Logic

"Return" is used specifically for going back to your **Base** (Home, Your Country, Your Office).

To connect your destination to the verb, we use the Directional Particle.

Destination
へ
Movement Verb

Pronunciation Alert!

Written as へ (he), but as a particle, it is pronounced as "e".

ぎんこう へ いきます。

Ginkou e ikimasu (I go to the bank).

うち へ かえります。

Uchi e kaerimasu (I return home).
