Travel Survival 203

Train Station Roleplay

1/6

Today you are leaving the hotel and using a train station in Japan. You will tell the station staff where you want to go, ask for the platform, and listen for time and price.

Today's Target

  • Say where you want to go.
  • Ask which platform to use.
  • Understand time and price.

Review Stack

すみません。

いくらですか。

わかりました。ありがとうございます。

Use に for your destination. Then choose 行きます for "I go" or 行きたいです for "I want to go."

Destination Formula

[ Place ]
に
行きます。

"I go to [Place]."

Go

東京駅 に 行きます。

I go to Tokyo Station.

Want to go

東京駅 に 行きたいです。

I want to go to Tokyo Station.

Keep It Simple

For this lesson, remember: destination + に + 行きたいです.

At a station, three short questions are very useful. Ask for the platform, the time, and the price.

Platform

何番ホームですか。

Which platform?

Time

何時ですか。

What time?

Price

いくらですか。

How much is it?

駅

2番ホームです。

Platform 2.

駅

10時15分です。

10:15.

駅

200円です。

200 yen.

Choose a destination and build the sentence.

Logic: [Destination] + に(ni) + 行きたいです(ikitai desu)

東京駅 Tokyo Station
京都 Kyoto
ホテル hotel
コンビニ convenience store

Your Sentence

東京駅 に 行きたいです。

Tokyo-eki ni ikitai desu.

Listen for three key details: platform number, departure time, and price. You do not need to understand every word.

Survival Listening

Catch the number first. Platform, time, and price are usually short number phrases.
